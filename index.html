Hello

I am one of today's foremost young violinists, with such a wide-ranging diversity of cross-cultural appeal as to have played a large part in defining what it means to be a true artist of the 21st century. Born in Galle Sri Lanka, I began to play the violin at the age of ten as a subject. I received a broad and thorough education at Southlands College, and now I am studying Computer Science undergraduate degree programme at the University of Colombo School of Computing.
